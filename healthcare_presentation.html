Key Insights

Revenue leadership

Cardiology drives the most revenue

Cardiology generated $420,800 in revenue and has the highest revenue per visit at $21,040. It is the most profitable department in the facility by a significant margin.

Financial risk

Oncology carries the highest financial risk

Oncology has both the highest average bill at $22,615 and the highest unpaid balance at $147,000. Patients in this department are more likely to be partially paid or unpaid.

Insurance concern

BlueCross covers most patients but pays the least

BlueCross insures the most patients (45) but has the lowest payment rate at 81.4% and the highest unpaid balance at $71,300. This relationship needs closer monitoring.

Collection strength

70.9% of patients are fully paid

The majority of patients have settled their bills in full. Only 1.7% are completely unpaid. The $222,800 gap is largely from partially paid accounts worth pursuing.

Smoking cost impact

Smokers cost the facility three times more

The average bill for smokers is $19,675 compared to $6,355 for non-smokers. While smokers represent only 17% of patients, they generate a disproportionate share of costs.

Capacity planning

Senior patients require significantly longer stays

Senior patients stay an average of 5.93 days compared to 1.00 day for young patients. Geriatrics has the longest average stay at 7.07 days, which has direct staffing and bed capacity implications.
